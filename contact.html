Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Smart Tips for Kids free to use?

Yes! Our basic platform is completely free. We offer premium features for a small monthly subscription, but all core educational content, games, and activities are available at no cost.

What age range is your content designed for?

Our content is designed for children ages 3-10. We categorize activities, games, and stories by age group to ensure they're developmentally appropriate. Each item is clearly marked with the recommended age range.

Do you have content for children with special needs?

Yes, we're committed to inclusivity. Many of our activities can be adapted for different learning needs. We're continuously working with special education experts to expand our accessible content. Contact us for specific recommendations.

Can I submit my own activity or game idea?

Absolutely! We love hearing from educators, parents, and creative minds. Use the contact form above to share your ideas. If we use your suggestion, we'll give you credit and a free premium subscription.

How can I get involved as a volunteer or contributor?

We welcome volunteers for content review, testing with children, and translation. We also accept guest blog posts from qualified educators and child development experts. Email us at contribute@smarttipsforkids.com for more information.
